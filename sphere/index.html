About the 10-20 System

The International 10-20 system is a standardized method for placing electrodes on the scalp for EEG recordings. The "10" and "20" refer to the distances between electrodes, which are either 10% or 20% of the total front-back or left-right distance of the skull. Connect your EEG device to automatically detect available channels.
