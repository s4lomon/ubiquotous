What is BCI?

Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) is a revolutionary technology that creates a direct communication pathway between the brain and external devices. By detecting and interpreting neural signals, BCI systems enable users to control computers, applications, and devices using only their thoughts. This technology has applications in healthcare, gaming, meditation, accessibility, and human augmentation. Using devices like the Muse EEG headband, we can capture brainwave patterns (Alpha, Beta, Theta, Delta, Gamma) and translate them into meaningful actions and insights.

Hardware

Muse EEG Headband

The Muse is a research-grade EEG device that captures brainwave activity in real-time. It features 4-7 sensors that detect electrical signals from your brain, enabling applications in meditation, focus training, and brain-computer interface research.
