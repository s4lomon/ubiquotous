APO Mind Control – Your Brain, Your Commands

Project Vision

Imagine controlling your smartphone and computer with just your thoughts. APO Mind Control is a groundbreaking concept application that will bridge Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) technology with your everyday digital life.

Using EEG devices like the Muse headband, this application aims to enable you to automate tasks across your existing applications – from sending messages and controlling media playback to launching apps and managing notifications – all through mind control patterns.
